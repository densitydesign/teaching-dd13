NEUTRALITY WAY

Considering how essential the Internet is, you might be wondering how net neutrality affects you on a daily basis. This set of rules protects everyday Internet users, from the whims of massive corporations looking to make an extra money. Net neutrality mandates companies to treat all Internet data as equal. Without Net Neutrality, Internet Service Providers can speed up, slow down, or hide any kind of internet content from users.
