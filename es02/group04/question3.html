Description

The network graph shows what are right now the different arguments that surround and go across the basic income topic. The graph is the network of all links in the section see also of the Wikipedia page respectively corresponding to the search items “basic income” and “global basic income”(to extend the point of view). We can infer that basic income is mostly an economic and social issue but it touch many different themes like politics, technology, philosophy, law, and many others. Colors show the different categories of arguments even some possible direct actor in the controversy and node dimension show the number of see also link in that page.

Protocol

The graph is made with Seealsology at the level 2 and modified with Gephy and sigma.js. Categories are selected through a qualitative analysis and a manual tagging with the Gephy filter tools.
